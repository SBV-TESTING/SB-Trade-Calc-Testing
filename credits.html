Site Help & How to use

SBV is a trade calculator for slap battles allowing you to calculate trades, and figure out if it is a win/fair/lose. Simply add items that are for your offer on the left side, and items for their offer on the right side. Win means you should take it, fair means the trade is even, while lose means you are losing value (don't take it unless you're okay with losing value).
